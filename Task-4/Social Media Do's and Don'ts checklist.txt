#🔏Checklist for DO's and Don'ts for responsible social media use
📌Do's checklist :

1.)Regularly check and update the privacy settings to control who sees your personal content.
2.)Only accept friend requests from people you personally know.
3.)Do not over share your personal information in your social media post.
4.)Use strong, unique passwords and enable two factor authentication`.
5.)Be respectful in comments, of people and do not spread hate speech.

📌Don'ts for Responsible Social Media Usage:

1.)Avoid posting your home address, phone number, or sensitive information.
2.)Avoid clicking links from unknown sources, even if they appear in messages from friends.
3.) Avoid posting real-time locations or travel plans to protect your safety.
4.)Avoid posting when you are angry, tired, or emotional to prevent regrets.
5.) Avoid using images or content that do not belong to you; use your own or properly licensed materials.
